Who I am

Hi! My name is Julia, I live in a small town by the Baltic Sea, named Klaipeda.

Art is my passion and from time to time I grab canvas and acrylic paint and create something colorful.

Enjoy the gallery!

Why colors?

Why to enjoy painting? It helps to clear one's mind and calm the soul.
Here are some inspirational quotes about art and painting.

The purpose of art is

washing the dust

of daily life off our souls.

Pablo Picasso

God is really only another artist.

He invented the giraffe, the elephant and the cat.

He has no real style, He just goes on trying other things.

Pablo Picasso

All children are artists.

The problem is

how to remain an artist

once he grows up.

Pablo Picasso
